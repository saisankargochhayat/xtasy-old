THEATRE PLAY COMPETITION
Rules:
1. This is a group event. Each team should have a minimum of 5 actors. In addition there can be 1 person in charge of music and a maximum of 3 people for lights.
2. The event will take place in 1 round.
3. Total time allotted for each team in the prelims is 30-40 minutes.
4. Language of the play can be English/Hindi. Mime is allowed.
5. The time duration includes performance time and stage setting time. The given time duration is from empty stage to empty stage.
6. The judge's decision will be final and binding.
7. The organizing team reserves the right to change or modify any of the rules.

Prizes: Winner- Rs. 5000         Runner up- Citation
