GOONJ
The Solo Singing Competition
Rules:
The participant can choose any of these two categories:
       • Eastern
       • Western
1. Each participant will be required to sing ONLY ONE song for 3 minutes.
2. The participant has an option of using an instrument to complement his/her song.
3. NO KARAOKE IS ALLOWED
4. Originality will be taken into account while scores are given out. So original compositions are welcome. But that does not guarantee higher scores.
5. Judges decision will be final and binding.
6. The organizing team reserves the right to change or modify any of the rules.

Prizes: Winner- Rs. 2000         Runner up- Rs. 1000  (for Eastern Category)
            Winner- Rs. 2000         Runner up- Rs. 1000  (for Western Category)
