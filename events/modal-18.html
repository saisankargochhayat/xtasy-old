SARGAM
The Duet Singing Competition
Rules:
This is a Duet Singing competition.
1. Each pair of participants will be allowed to sing ONLY ONE song for 3-4 minutes.
2. Every group must bring their own Karaoke Track. Use of Karaoke is must.
3. Language of performances have been restricted to Hindi and English only.
4. Judges decision will be final and binding.
5. Judging will be based on the coordination among the singers and tonal quality.

Prizes: Winner- Rs. 2000         Runner up- Citation
