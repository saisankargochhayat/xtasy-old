REFLEX
Instant Theatre Play Competition
Rules:
1. This is a group event. Each team should have a minimum of 5 members and maximum 7 members.
2. The event will take place in two rounds- Prelims and Finale.
3. Each team will be given some phrases, scenarios, charaters or situation which they have to represent creatively in 10 minutes.
4. Total time allotted for each team shall be 10 minutes.
5. The time duration for prelims and finals includes performance time and stage setting time. The given time duration is from empty stage to empty stage.

Prizes: Winner- Rs. 2000         Runner up- Citation
